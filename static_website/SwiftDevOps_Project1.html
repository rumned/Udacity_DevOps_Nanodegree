Mantis Shrimp

Supersonic Puncher with Super Color Vision

  • Scientific Name: Stomatopoda
  • Average Length: 10 cm (3.9 in)
  • Average Lifespan: 2-7 years
  • Habitat: Shallow, tropical and subtropical marine habitats.

Using its 12 types of color receptors in its eyes, plus UV light and polarized light receptors, it spots prey on the sea floor, then hunts them with its raptorial apppendages. These could accelerate with the velocity of a bullet from a gun under such a short time that the water around them boils almost immediately through supercavitation. One punch could instantly break a crab's shells.
